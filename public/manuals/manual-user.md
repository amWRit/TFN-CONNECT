## Features & How-To

### 1. Explore the Community
**Find and connect with others:**
- Go to the **People** page from the main menu to browse all TFN alumni, staff, and leadership.
- Use the search bar to find people by name, cohort, or role.
- Click on a profile to view details, see their journey, and connect.
- You can bookmark profiles to revisit them later.

### 2. Find Jobs & Opportunities
**Discover roles and programs:**
- Visit the **Jobs** or **Opportunities** pages to see all available listings.
- Use filters (location, type, status, etc.) to narrow your search.
- Click on a job or opportunity to view full details, requirements, and how to apply.
- Mark items as "Interested" to keep track, or click the bookmark icon to save them.

### 3. Community Feed
**Share and interact:**
- Go to the **Feed** page to see posts from the community.
- Click "New Post" to share your own update, win, question, or reflection.
- Like and comment on posts to engage with others.
- Edit or delete your own posts from your activity page.

### 4. Bookmarks
**Save and organize:**
- Bookmark people, jobs, opportunities, and posts by clicking the bookmark icon (ðŸ”–) anywhere you see it.
- Access all your bookmarks from the profile menu under "My Bookmarks."
- Remove bookmarks at any time from the bookmarks page.

### 5. Profile & Settings
**Personalize your experience:**
- Click your profile icon and select "Profile" to view or edit your information.
- Update your photo, bio, work experience, and education.
- Go to "Settings" to manage notifications, subscriptions, and privacy options.

### 6. Events
**Stay involved:**
- Visit the **Events** page to see upcoming and past events.
- Click on an event for details, agenda, and location.
- RSVP to events and see who else is attending.

### 7. Activity
**Track your engagement:**
- Access your activity page from the profile menu.
- See a summary of your posts, jobs, opportunities, bookmarks, and interests.
- Use this page to manage your contributions and follow up on saved items.
